GAME INSTRUCTIONS

trials of eternal bonding

•  Each table in this room is an individual raid party.

•  Everyone is given a role based on the color of their envelopes.

•  Those that have the blue envelopes are assigned the TANK role card. The Tanks’ job is to draw the enemies’ attention away. Show your card to everyone at your table.

•  Those that have the green envelopes are assigned the HEALER role card. The Healers’ job is to protect and heal their party from harm. Show your card to the party.

•  Lastly, those that have the red envelopes are assigned the DPS role card. They are the main attackers. Their job is to attack the enemy as fast as possible. Show your card to the party.

•  Keep your card close. You will be using it to play the game.

•  There is a white envelope on each table. Inside, there is a paper with four questions, a pencil and one gold card.

•  Read your role cards and the conditions of the gold card. Talk with your party and assign one person to write down your collective answers to the four questions.

•  The game will follow in the same order as the questions listed.

•  Each answer will require the right roles to STAND UP and SHOW THEIR CARDS.

•  Roleplay is encouraged. Feel free to act out your role.

•  Participation is optional. If there is anyone who does not want to participate, others in the table can hold more than one role card.

•  If there is a table that collectively does not want to participate, let the Game Master or the MC know.
